## Story
Well, it's been fun riding my first [beginner bike](/projects/ninja-500), but it's time to upgrade 😎.

I liked the Ninja 500, but I knew when I bought it that I wouldn't keep it. It's fun, but I never really **loved** the way it looked, or how it sounded.

My new bike, however, solved those problems for me.

I purchased a lightly used (5k mi) 2022 Yamaha XSR 900 in "Legend Blue" off of Marketplace.

It has a **890cc** 3 cylinder engine, making **117**hp and **68**lb-ft of torque. A quickshifter up and down, multiple riding modes, wheelie control, and a nice sounding exhaust made me giddy when I test rode it.
